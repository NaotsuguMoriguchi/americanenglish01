A Child Can Learn Any Language
Every sound of every languag is within every child. So,
what happens with adults? We learn our native language
and stop listening for the sounds that we never hear, then
we lose the ability to hear those sounds. Later, when we
study another language, we learn a lot of spelling rules
that take us still further away from the real sound of
English.
